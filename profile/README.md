# Steiner Lab 🛰️🌎📊

**Remote Sensing for Surface Hydrology & Environmental Monitoring**

---

## What We Do

We develop satellite-based methods to monitor floods, wetlands, and ecosystems—turning radar and thermal imagery into actionable science for climate adaptation and disaster response.

**Research Focus Areas:**
- 🌊 **Flood Monitoring** — SAR-based flood extent mapping, rapid response systems
- 🌡️ **Urban Heat** — Thermal remote sensing for environmental justice applications  
- 🌿 **Wetlands & Carbon** — Inundation dynamics, methane emissions, peatland monitoring
- ❄️ **Arctic Hydrology** — Permafrost, freeze/thaw cycles, surface water dynamics
